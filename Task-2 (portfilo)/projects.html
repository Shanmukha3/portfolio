Project-1:


Human Activity Recognition using DL

"Human Activity Recognition using Deep Learning is a technology that utilizes advanced neural networks to automatically identify and categorize human actions and behaviors based on sensor data. It has applications in fitness tracking, healthcare, and security, enabling real-time monitoring and analysis of activities such as walking, running, and more. Deep learning models, like Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), play a key role in capturing complex patterns from sensor data. This technology enhances our ability to understand and respond to human activities, making it invaluable in various industries."

Project-2:


Plant Disease Detection Using ML and DL

Plant disease detection using ML and AI involves the application of machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques to identify and diagnose diseases in plants. This innovative approach utilizes image recognition, sensor data, and advanced algorithms to analyze plant health, helping farmers detect issues early and take timely corrective measures. It enhances agricultural productivity by reducing crop losses, promoting sustainable farming practices, and optimizing resource utilization. By automating disease identification, it offers a cost-effective and efficient solution to address the challenges of food security and crop management in the agriculture sector.
